About Our Hospital Branches

Our hospital has multiple branches located in different regions, each providing top-notch healthcare services. Here are the details of our main branches:

Colombo Branch

The Colombo Branch is our flagship hospital, providing a wide range of services including emergency care, cardiology, and advanced surgeries. Our skilled staff and modern facilities ensure that patients receive the best care possible.

Negombo Branch

The Negombo Branch focuses on maternal and child care, providing specialized services to expectant mothers, infants, and children. With dedicated pediatricians and obstetricians, we offer personalized healthcare for families.

Kandy Branch

The Kandy Branch offers advanced rehabilitation and physical therapy services. Equipped with the latest medical technology and highly trained professionals, this branch specializes in recovery and physical therapy treatments.
